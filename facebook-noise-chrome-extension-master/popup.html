Facebook® Noise is a small voluntary misinformation experiment aimed at adding noise to the system and seeing if it is possible to say yes so many times that it becomes a no.
Every time you visit Facebook®, Facebook® Noise will randomly and freely distribute likes across your timeline to friends' comments, posts, photos, etc. obfuscating your real social data and exploring new strategies of taking back privacy through acts of performed openness.
By using the Facebook® Noise Google Chrome extension, you assume all legal liability and agree to voluntarily participate in this group experiment at your own risk. That being said, don't be afraid to throw yourself in front of the machine.
